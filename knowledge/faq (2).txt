MIL MAIDS FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


Q: What services does Mil Maids offer?
A: Mil Maids offers Standard Cleaning, Deep Cleaning, and Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning for residential homes.


Q: What is included in a standard cleaning?
A: A Standard Clean includes routine cleaning tasks such as dusting surfaces, vacuuming floors, sweeping and mopping floors, cleaning sinks, cleaning bathrooms, and wiping kitchen surfaces.


Q: What is the difference between a standard clean and a deep clean?
A: A Deep Clean includes everything in a Standard Clean but with additional detailed cleaning tasks designed for homes that require extra attention.


Q: Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
A: No. Many customers provide entry instructions and return after the cleaning is completed.


Q: Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?
A: No. Mil Maids cleaning teams bring their own professional cleaning supplies and equipment.


Q: Can I add extra services to my cleaning?
A: Yes. Add-on services such as oven cleaning, refrigerator cleaning, interior cabinet cleaning, and window cleaning can be added when booking.


Q: How is pricing determined?
A: Pricing depends on the cleaning service selected, the square footage of the home, and any add-on services requested.


Q: Do you clean inside appliances?
A: Yes. Services such as oven cleaning, refrigerator cleaning, and dishwasher cleaning can be added as optional add-on services.


Q: Do you offer move-out cleaning?
A: Yes. Mil Maids offers Move-In and Move-Out cleaning services designed to prepare a home for new occupants.


Q: What if my home is extremely dirty or cluttered?
A: Homes with excessive clutter, heavy buildup, or large amounts of pet hair may require a custom quote from a Mil Maids team member.


Q: How do I book a cleaning?
A: Customers can request a quote online or contact Mil Maids directly by phone or email.


Q: What areas do you serve?
A: Mil Maids serves multiple military communities across the United States. See the service areas section for specific locations.
