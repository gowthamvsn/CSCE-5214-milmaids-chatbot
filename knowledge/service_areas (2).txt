HOW TO DETERMINE SERVICE AVAILABILITY

If a customer asks whether Mil Maids serves their location:

1. Check if the city is within one of the primary service regions.
2. If the city is near a listed military installation or service region, inform the customer that service may be available.
3. If the location is unclear, inform the customer that Mil Maids services the areas surrounding Fort Hood, TX. JBSA, TX. and Fort Bragg, NC.
4. If the customer is still unsure whether their home is in an area serviced by Mil Maids, direct the customer to contact Mil Maids.
